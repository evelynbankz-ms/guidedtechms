Company Overview

Guided Tech Solutions of Mississippi LLC is a Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB) offering advanced IT solutions across cloud computing, network engineering, and enterprise IT support.

We design, migrate, and operate secure infrastructure environments that enable reliability, compliance, and operational excellence.

At a Glance

  • Business Type: SDVOSB
  • Location: Clinton, MS
  • Focus: Cloud, Network, DevOps
  • Delivery: Full lifecycle execution

Core Competencies

Cloud Architecture & Migration

Lift-and-shift and refactoring of legacy workloads to AWS and Azure using Terraform, Ansible, and CI/CD automation.

Infrastructure as Code (IaC)

Secure landing zone design and Terraform module development for multi-account AWS environments.

Network Engineering

Secure, compliant network architectures using Palo Alto, F5 BIG-IP, Cisco, and Fortinet technologies.

Hybrid Infrastructure Optimization

Colo-to-cloud transitions, VPNs, Direct Connect, and scalable disaster recovery solutions.

Compliance-First Design

HIPAA, FERPA, and NIST 800-53 aligned deployments with audit logging, encryption, and access controls.

DevOps-Driven Delivery

Secure automation pipelines, observability, runbooks, and operational readiness.

Differentiators

  • Veteran-Owned, SDVOSB Certified
  • Real-world cloud migration with zero downtime
  • Custom Terraform modules reducing deployment time
  • Single-lead, full lifecycle project ownership
  • Dual-strength architecture and operations team

Why Clients Choose Us

We combine technical depth with operational accountability. Our work prioritizes uptime, compliance, and long-term sustainability — not just implementation.
